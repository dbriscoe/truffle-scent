Professional serving inspiration

These ideas are designed to help customers order with confidence. Each plate uses the pantry products in a practical, polished way.

Recipe idea

Truffle Fettuccine for Two

A glossy, elegant pasta built around Salsa al Tartufo, fettuccine and a restrained finish of truffle oil.

  • Cook fettuccine until just tender.
  • Warm truffle salsa gently with butter or olive oil.
  • Loosen with pasta water, then finish with Parmesan.
Tip: Keep the sauce silky rather than heavy; pasta water is the secret.

Recipe idea

Black Truffle Risotto

A slow, comforting plate for evenings when the food should feel generous and restaurant-adjacent.

  • Toast rice in olive oil.
  • Add warm stock gradually.
  • Finish with truffle salsa and a few drops of black truffle oil.
Tip: Add truffle at the end so the aroma stays lifted.

Recipe idea

Truffle Toast & Chilli

A quick appetiser with warm bread, truffle salsa, visible truffle slices and a tiny chilli spark.

  • Toast bread until crisp outside.
  • Spread a thin layer of truffle salsa.
  • Finish with carpaccio and a little peperoncino.
Tip: Excellent as a welcome bite before pasta or wine.

Recipe idea

Cheese & Truffle Board

A simple hosting board made special with white truffle condiment, carpaccio slices and good bread.

  • Choose two cheeses with different textures.
  • Add truffle condiment in a small dish.
  • Serve with olives, bread and a light drizzle of olive oil.
Tip: Let cheese sit briefly at room temperature for better flavour.

LEE’s pantry notes

Small touches that make food feel elegant.

01

Finish, don’t flood

Truffle oils and condiments are most elegant when used at the end. Start small, taste, then add more only if the dish asks for it.

02

Use pasta water

A splash of starchy water helps truffle salsa, pesto and oil-based sauces cling beautifully to pasta.

03

Build a gift bundle

Pair one sauce, one pasta and one finishing oil for an easy, thoughtful gourmet gift.

04

Let aroma rise

Serve warm truffle dishes immediately so the aroma reaches the table at its best.
