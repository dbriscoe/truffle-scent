Signature pasta

Truffle Fettuccine for Two

Use fettuccine, Salsa al Tartufo, a little butter and a finishing drop of white truffle oil. Loosen with pasta water until glossy, then serve immediately.

  • Best for date night or a quiet dinner
  • Finish with Parmesan and black pepper
  • Keep the sauce warm, never boiling

Black Truffle Risotto

Build a creamy risotto with warm stock, then fold in truffle salsa near the end. Finish with black truffle oil just before serving.

  • Serve in warm bowls

LEE’s pantry notes

Small tips for a more elegant result.

Luxury ingredients work best when they are used with restraint, warmth and good timing.

Finish, don’t fry

Truffle oils are most aromatic when added at the end. Use everyday olive oil for cooking, then finish with truffle oil for fragrance.

Save pasta water

A splash of starchy pasta water helps truffle salsa, pesto and olive oil cling to pasta with a glossy, restaurant-style finish.

Warm the plate

Warm plates keep pasta and risotto luxurious for longer, especially when serving creamy or truffle-led dishes.

Build a gift bundle

For gifting, pair one hero product with one everyday product: truffle salsa with pasta, or carpaccio with a cheese board condiment.
